# Privacy Policy — Eurognosi Homework Sheet Generator

_Last updated: 14 July 2026_

This browser extension ("the extension") is provided for use by staff of
eurognosi-fni.com. This policy explains, in plain terms, what the extension
does and does not do with your data.

## Summary

**The extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or
user data whatsoever.** Everything it does happens locally, inside your own
browser, on your own computer.

## What the extension accesses

To do its job, the extension runs only on the school's Wix Groups pages
(`https://www.eurognosi-fni.com/group/*`). On those pages it reads the text of
homework posts so that, when you click the **📄 Homework Sheet** button, it can
generate a printable Word (`.docx`) document from that text.

- It does **not** run on any other website or domain.
- It reads post text only at the moment you use it, only to build the document.

## What the extension does with that data

- The Word document is generated **entirely within your browser** and downloaded
  directly to your computer.
- No page content, post text, document, or any other information is sent to us,
  to any server, or to any third party.
- The extension has **no backend, no analytics, no tracking, and no accounts**.
- It requests no browsing history, tabs, storage, cookies, or other
  browser-permission access.

## Data sharing and sale

Because the extension collects no data, there is nothing to share or sell. No
data is ever transferred to third parties for any purpose.

## Changes to this policy

If the extension's behavior ever changes in a way that affects this policy, this
document will be updated and the "Last updated" date above changed.

## Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to the extension's maintainer via the
project's repository: https://github.com/roydestroy/homework-sheets
